Privacy Policy — Volume Control

Last updated: 2026-07-09

Volume Control collects no data. None. It runs entirely on your computer and never sends anything anywhere.

What we collect

Nothing. No personal data, no analytics, no telemetry, no crash reports, no identifiers. The extension makes no network requests of any kind.

What is stored locally

Only your per-tab volume and mute settings, saved with Chrome's chrome.storage.local API on your own device. Each entry is keyed by a browser tab and is removed when that tab closes. This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Permissions

  • tabs — read tab titles, icons and audible state to show them in the mixer.
  • storage — remember each tab's volume/mute for the tab's lifetime.
  • scripting and host access — apply the volume you set to the page's audio.

These permissions are used solely to control audio locally. They are never used to read page content for any other purpose, and nothing is transmitted off your device.

Third parties

None. There are no third-party services, SDKs, or trackers.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date. Material changes will be reflected in the extension's store listing.
